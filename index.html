Rice Advanced Engineering Systems and Research (RAESR)

Founded by Eric Rice in 2014, RAESR is a high-end automotive design and technology startup focused on crafting a limited number of hand-built electric and hybrid-electric hypercars. The sum of our extensive R&D in high-performance electric powertrains, cutting-edge prototyping processes, and energy storage systems culminates in the world's premier next-generation electric hypercar, the Tachyon Speed.
