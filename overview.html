RAESR Tachyon Speed

RAESR Tachyon Speed

The Tachyon Speed is a street-legal prototype electric hypercar developed by RAESR Automotive, an ambitious and passionate team with the mission to bring the ultimate in road and track performance to a low volume production of extreme electric vehicles.

Designed and manufactured in Southern California by RAESR, the Tachyon Speed is a proof of concept that showcases an assortment of distinctive features. These include outrageously low (39.5") and wide (81") dimensions, a 6-motor 1200+hp proprietary electric powertrain, twin double hinged doors, and a jet-fighter style canopy.
